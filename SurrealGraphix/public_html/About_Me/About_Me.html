About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Joel Youngberg. Ever since I can remember I have loved art and computers. From the early inspiration that I received from my older sister Kim McWhorter, who is an excellent painter, to the urgings of my teachers in school, I have always loved the union between art and computers. Many people believe that a person can only be a right-brained, artistic, creative, designer type OR a left-brained, logical, mathematical, scientific type of person. While it is true that one of the two types will come more naturally to particular individuals; I say “Why not challenge yourself and use your whole brain, not just half of it!” The intersection of art and the computer have created marvelous new things in our day, and opened career fields that never existed in the past. We now have the web, video games, and movies with special effects that were unheard of a few decades ago. Exciting careers in areas such as web design, 3D game design, and computer-generated special effects are now commonplace. With the rapid improvements in technology that we see constantly, I look forward to what the future holds for us in the world of computer-generated art.
